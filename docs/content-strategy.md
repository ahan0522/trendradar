# TrendRadar Content Strategy

## Content Goal

TrendRadar content should build trust before selling.

The goal is to teach the market that TrendRadar is not another AI news summary tool. It is a signal research system that tracks whether market signals become real.

## Core Message

Discover market signals before they become consensus.

## Content Layers

### Free Layer

Purpose:

- Build awareness
- Educate users
- Show daily signal thinking
- Attract investors, traders, and research-minded followers

Includes:

- Daily Signals
- Weekly Signals
- Market education posts
- Selected signal teasers

Does not include:

- Full watchlist
- Full backtest
- Full timeline
- Full Signal Ledger access

### Paid Layer

Purpose:

- Provide complete research value
- Save research time
- Give access to the accumulated Signal Ledger

Includes:

- Full research reports
- Full signal timeline
- Investment basket
- Backtest
- Lessons learned
- Signal database
- API in the future
- Institutional research in the future

## Daily Signals

Cadence:

- Daily after US market close data is available
- Taiwan morning or midday can be used for processing
- Post manually at first

Daily question:

What new market signals appeared today?

Suggested format:

```text
Daily Signal Brief

Main Signal:
AI Power Infrastructure

Why it matters:
Data center expansion is shifting the bottleneck from compute to power.

Signal DNA:
News: 70
Price: 45
Supply Chain: 80
Company Activity: 60
Persistence: 65
Beneficiary Clarity: 75

What to watch:
Transformer orders, grid equipment demand, and data center power capex.
```

## Weekly Signals

Cadence:

- Sunday

Weekly question:

Which signals were added, validated, failed, or gained momentum this week?

Suggested sections:

- New signals
- Rising signals
- Validated signals
- Failed signals
- Signals to watch next week

## Monthly Validation Report

Cadence:

- Monthly, but not necessarily fully public

Purpose:

- Show credibility
- Present complete case studies
- Support paid tier

Suggested content:

- Executive summary
- Signal Ledger statistics
- Best signals
- Failed signals
- Average alpha
- Average lead time
- Case study deep dives
- Lessons learned

## Social Platforms

### Threads

Use for:

- Short market signal thoughts
- Daily brief summaries
- Educational threads

Tone:

- Clear
- Research-focused
- Not hype-driven

### X

Use for:

- Market signal commentary
- Charts
- Threaded case studies

Tone:

- More concise
- More data-driven

### Instagram

Use for:

- Signal cards
- Market map cards
- Weekly summary images

Suggested card types:

1. Single Signal Card
2. Market Map Card
3. Signal DNA Card
4. Timeline Card
5. Weekly Review Card

## What To Avoid

Avoid:

- Buy / sell language
- Price targets
- Hype titles
- Claiming predictive certainty
- Hiding failed signals
- Overproducing content before case studies are strong

Preferred wording:

- "Worth researching"
- "Signal detected"
- "Potential beneficiary"
- "Needs validation"
- "Pending outcome"

Avoid wording:

- "Must buy"
- "Guaranteed winner"
- "AI predicts"
- "Explosive stock"

## First Content MVP

Before building automated posting, build a Daily Signal Brief generator.

It should generate:

- Website brief
- Threads copy
- X copy
- IG card copy

Posting should remain manual until product-market fit becomes clearer.
